Final Project: Space Invaders

Space Invaders - Link to Code

For my final CSC 106 project, my partner and I created our own version of an old arcade classic - Space Invaders! To start the game, click the "PLAY" button. Once the game starts use your left and right arrow keys on your keyboard to move the spaceship, and the space bar to shoot. The goal is to shoot each of the invaders before they either shoot you or reach the bottom of the screen. You have three lives! To play again, just refresh your browser.
